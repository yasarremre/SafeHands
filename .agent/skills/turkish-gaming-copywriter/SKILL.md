## STRUCTURE (MANDATORY)

Always follow this exact structure:

### 1. MAIN HOOK TITLE (H2)

- First headline MUST be formatted as H2.
- Emotional, epic, and action-driven.
- Must include full product name.

Rules:

- Introduce the game world.

  - Dominance
  - Progress
  - Power
  - Uniqueness
